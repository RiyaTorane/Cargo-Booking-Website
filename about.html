About Us

Buying a plane ticket online is easy. With a few clicks, you have your ticket in your inbox. But when it comes to booking shipments on the very same airplane, the experience is nowhere near as easy. It takes days of phone calls and back and forth before you can book a cargo shipment. RT Cargo is changing that. Our vision is cargo booking as easily as passengers travel. Search. Compare. Book. It can be that easy! Over 1,800 freight forwarders across 90 countries use RT Cargo for eBookings, instant quoting, digital freight negotiation, sales portals, rate management software, air cargo rate search across more than 370 airlines, and more.
